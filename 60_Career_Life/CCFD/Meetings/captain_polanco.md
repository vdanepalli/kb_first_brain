### Personal Leave - Scheduling

1. Aug 1 to Jul 31; Goes away if not used. 
2. Fixed Amount is credited; 40 hour employees get 40 hours; 54 hour employees get 60 hours. 
3. Can take 12 hour or 24 hour blocks; Can be combined with VAC. 
4. Action Request for PL -> Captain -> BC -> Shift Commander
5. Past Schedule does not ususally change. 
6. Todays Schedule can be changed. 
7. By tomorrow, Today's Schedule should be accurate. 
8. Only up to 5 personnel can take PL on any single day. 
9. Email Captain -> Say I need X PL hours on Y date.
